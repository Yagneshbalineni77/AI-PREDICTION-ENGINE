Breaking News: Tech giant "CyberDyne Systems" has just announced that their flagship AI smartphone, the "Nexus 9", will no longer support any third-party apps starting next month. All users will be forced to use only CyberDyne's proprietary apps. Furthermore, the phone's battery life has been reported to degrade significantly after the newest compulsory software update.
 
Meanwhile, rival company "OpenTech" has announced a massive 50% discount on their open-source alternative phone, the "Liberty X". OpenTech's CEO tweeted: "Your phone is your property. You should decide what runs on it."
